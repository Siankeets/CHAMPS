WHO WE ARE

About BCBP

The Brotherhood of Christian Businessmen and Professionals is a Catholic Christian organization dedicated to building Christ-centered leaders in business and professional life. We promote faith, ethical leadership, integrity, excellence, and service.

Our Vision

Bringing Christ into the marketplace and winning the marketplace for Christ.

Our Mission

To form Christian leaders who influence society through faith, professional excellence, justice, integrity, and service.
